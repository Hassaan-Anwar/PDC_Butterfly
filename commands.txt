Normal Sequential: 
g++ -Wall -O2 -o butterfly attempt1.cpp
./butterfly

OpenMP only:
 g++ -std=c++11 -fopenmp -O3 openmp.cpp -o quick
 ./quick


 OpenMPI only:
mpic++ -std=c++14 -Wall -O3 -fopenmp -o mpi_butterfly openmpi.cpp -lmetis  
mpiexec -n 4 ./mpi_butterfly


OpenMPI And OpenMP:
 mpic++ -fopenmp -Wall -O3 -std=c++17 -o new openmpi_openmp.cpp -lmetis
 mpiexec -n 4 ./new
